<h2 style={{ fontSize: '22px', marginBottom: '8px' }}>
  Overview
</h2>

<p style={{ fontSize: '15px', lineHeight: '1.8', color: '#374151' }}>
  The Market Report API returns a single point-in-time snapshot that aggregates
  six independent market views into one response: AI-highlighted news, today's
  rising and falling tokens, market sentiment, key trend events, and major
  events tonight.
</p>

<p style={{ fontSize: '15px', lineHeight: '1.8' }}>
  Each section comes from its own upstream batch and surfaces its own
  <code>publish_time</code>; sections do not share a batch timestamp. Missing
  sections render as <code>null</code> or empty arrays rather than failing the
  whole response.
</p>

<Note>
  All outputs are generated as a <strong>point-in-time snapshot</strong> and are intended
  for informational purposes only. This API does not provide investment advice.
</Note>

<h3 style={{ marginTop: '24px' }}>What you get</h3>

<ul style={{ fontSize: '15px', lineHeight: '1.8' }}>
  <li><strong>must_know_news</strong> — AI-highlighted macro/market news digest</li>
  <li><strong>rise_tokens</strong> / <strong>drop_tokens</strong> — today's biggest gainers and losers with reasons</li>
  <li><strong>market_sentiment</strong> — Fear &amp; Greed index plus UP/FLAT/DOWN price-fluctuation counts</li>
  <li><strong>key_trend_events</strong> — major themes shaping the Korean crypto market</li>
  <li><strong>market_events</strong> — scheduled events for the rest of the day (FOMC, rate decisions, etc.)</li>
</ul>

</div>
