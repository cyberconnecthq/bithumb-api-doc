<h2 style={{ fontSize: "22px", marginBottom: 8 }}>Overview</h2>

<p style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>

</p>

<p style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  Overall score = <code>Round(Average(6 individual metric scores), 1)</code>.
</p>

<p style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  Only <code>date</code> (optional, YYYY-MM-DD). If omitted, returns the most recent data.
